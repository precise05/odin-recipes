Pulissery

Description

Pulissery is a tangy Kerala yogurt curry with coconut and mild spices, often cooked with pumpkin or ash gourd and served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1–2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 cup pumpkin or ash gourd, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1–2 dried red chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt with turmeric and some water; set aside.
  2. Cook pumpkin or ash gourd until tender.
  3. Grind coconut with green chilies to a paste; add to vegetables.
  4. Slowly stir in the yogurt mixture; simmer gently.
  5. Prepare tempering with oil, mustard seeds, red chilies, fenugreek, and curry leaves; pour over curry.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
