Parippu

Description

Paruppu Curry is a simple and nutritious South Indian lentil curry made with moong dal or toor dal, tempered with spices, and cooked with coconut for a rich flavor. It is often served with rice and makes a comforting everyday meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup moong dal or toor dal
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1â€“2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A few curry leaves
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 4 cups water

Steps

  1. Rinse the dal and cook it with turmeric in water until soft and mushy.
  2. Add chopped tomato, slit green chilies, and salt; simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. If using, grind grated coconut into a paste and add to the dal; mix well.
  4. Heat oil for tempering, add mustard seeds, cumin, dried red chilies, and curry leaves; fry until aromatic.
  5. Pour the tempering into the dal and stir gently.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
